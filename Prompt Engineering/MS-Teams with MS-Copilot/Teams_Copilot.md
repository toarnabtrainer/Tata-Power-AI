# **MS-Teams with MS-CoPilot**

Using **Microsoft Copilot in MS Teams** takes your collaboration game to the next level by integrating AI directly into chats, meetings, and teamwork. It helps summarize conversations, extract action items, prepare meeting agendas, and even answer questions based on organizational knowledge — all inside Teams.

---

## 🛠️ **Actions You Can Do with MS Copilot in Microsoft Teams**

### 🎤 1. **Summarize Meetings in Real-Time or Afterward**
- Get **automatic meeting recaps**, including:
  - Key discussion points
  - Action items
  - Assigned responsibilities
  - Decisions made

Example:  
*“What did we decide about the marketing budget in today’s meeting?”*

**Note:**
*To access a Teams meeting transcript, go to the meeting event in your calendar, click "Recordings & Transcripts", and then download the transcript as a .docx or .vtt file.*

---

### 📅 2. **Prepare for Meetings**
- Generate **agendas, suggested questions**, and background summaries before meetings based on calendar invites, past chats, and shared docs.

Example:  
*“Prepare a meeting brief for my 2 PM with the product team.”*

---

### 🧾 3. **Follow-Up Automatically**
- Copilot can draft **follow-up messages or emails** after a meeting or discussion.
- Include reminders, summaries, or next steps.

---

### 💬 4. **Summarize Chat Threads**
- Quickly catch up on long chat threads or channel messages.

Example:  
*“Summarize the last 3 days of discussion in the #sales channel.”*

---

### 📂 5. **Pull Context from Documents & Emails**
- Ask Copilot to find and summarize info from linked files, previous meetings, emails, or Teams messages.

Example:  
*“What did Alex say about the Q3 goals in the last meeting?”*

---

### ✅ 6. **Track Action Items & Tasks**
- Identify and list tasks mentioned in meetings or chats.
- Integrate with Microsoft To Do and Planner.

Example:  
*“What are the open tasks from our last sprint review?”*

---

### 📊 7. **Create Visual Summaries or Reports**
- Ask Copilot to prepare visual content like charts, slides, or bullet lists based on the meeting discussion or notes.

---

### 🧠 8. **Answer Organizational Questions**
- Ask Copilot questions like:
  - “Who is the PM on Project Phoenix?”
  - “What’s the latest status update from the engineering team?”

(It pulls from Teams messages, SharePoint, OneDrive, and more.)

---

## ✅ **Advantages of Using MS Copilot in Teams**

| Feature | Advantage |
|--------|-----------|
| **Efficiency** | Spend less time taking notes, writing follow-ups, and managing meetings. |
| **Clarity** | Stay aligned with summarized threads and decisions. |
| **Preparedness** | Come to meetings with background briefs and suggested agendas. |
| **Knowledge Discovery** | Ask questions and get answers from across your org’s data. |
| **Collaboration** | Keeps everyone in sync with automatic recaps and assigned action items. |
| **Productivity** | Streamlines workflows without leaving Teams. |
| **Time-saving** | No need to scroll through endless messages or rewatch meeting recordings. |

---

### 🔥 Example Prompts to Use with Copilot in Teams

- *“Summarize the last team meeting and list action items.”*
- *“What did we decide about the product launch date?”*
- *“Draft a follow-up message for today’s meeting with the HR team.”*
- *“What files were shared in the last budget review meeting?”*
