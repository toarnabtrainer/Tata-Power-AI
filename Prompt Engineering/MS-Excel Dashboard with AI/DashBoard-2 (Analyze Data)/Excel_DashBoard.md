## DYNAMIC BUSINESS DASHBOARD - STEP BY STEP

### Follow the following steps to create a DYNAMIC BUSINESS DASHBOARD

<hr>

- 1️⃣ **Gather your business data.**
    - ✅ Business data available in **Sample-Superstore.xlsx** ➡️ **Orders** worksheet.

<hr>

- 2️⃣ **Creation of required Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts.**
    - ✅ Go for Tab **Data** ➡️ Group **Data tools** ➡️ **Analyze Data** create following **"Pivot Tables"** and **"Pivot Charts"** in separate worksheets.
        - ☑️ **Total 'Profit' By 'Region' in Doughnut Graph** (ProfitByRegion).
        - ☑️ **Total 'Sales' by year and month of 'Order Date' in Clustered Column** (SalesByYearMonth).
        - ☑️ **Total 'Sales' by 'Ship Mode' in Bar Chart** (SalesByShipMode).
        - ☑️ **Total 'Profit' by 'Customer Name' in Line Chart** (ProfitByCustomer).
        - ☑️ **Total 'Sales' by 'Sub-Category' in Pie Chart** (SalesBySub-Category).

<hr>

- 3️⃣ **Create a new "Dashboard" worksheet.**
    - ✅ Create a new worksheet "Dashboard"
    - ✅ Copy all the five "Pivot Charts" to this worksheet, and then rearrange and resize and place them.
    - ✅ Go for Tab **Page Layout** ➡️ **Colors** ➡️ **Orange Red**
    - ✅ Go for Tab **Page Layout** ➡️ **Background** ➡️ **Bing Image Search** ➡️ **Search for "Paper"** ➡️ **Brown Paper**
    - ✅ Enable **"Data Labels"** to all **Pivot Charts**.
    - ✅ Put the proper background color and required formatting to all **Pivot Charts**.
    - ✅ Do the needful for all currency formatting.

<hr>

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/546ec335-fae4-4411-a63c-3678611a4957)

- 4️⃣ **Dashboard Caption.**
    - ✅ Go for Tab **Insert** ➡️ **Shapes** ➡️ **Rounded Corner Rectangle**.
    - ✅ Put the proper **color**, caption **"DYNAMIC BUSINESS DASHBOARD"**
    - ✅ Insert a **Logo Image** (Logo.jpg) in that shape.

<hr>

- 5️⃣ **Insert Slicers.**
    - ✅ Insert following Slicers.
        - ☑️ Insert Slicer on attribute **Segment** (Left Top-1).
        - ☑️ Insert Slicer on attribute **Category** (Left Top-2).
        - ☑️ Insert Slicer on attribute **Region** (Left Top-3).
        - ☑️ Insert Slicer on attribute **Ship Mode** (Left Bottom).
        - ☑️ Insert Slicer on attribute **Years** (Top Middle).
        - ☑️ Insert Slicer on attribute **Months** (Top Right).
    - ✅ Establish **"Report Connections"** of all the six **"Slicers"** with all five **"Pivot Charts"**.
    - ✅ Format and align all the six **"Slicers"**
